Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how I'm the Commish ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and device identifier.
  • Email Address: If you choose to provide an email address for notifications, we collect and store it. Email is optional and used solely for sending notifications you've opted into.
  • Contest Data: Information about contests you create or join, including picks, scores, and rankings.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in contest chat rooms.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect device identifiers for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, including features accessed and interactions.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we collect device tokens to send you notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process your picks and calculate scores and rankings
  • Enable contest features including chat and leaderboards
  • Send you notifications about your contests (with your consent)
  • Respond to your questions and provide support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, picks (after lock), and rankings are visible to other members of your contests.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services for hosting (MongoDB Atlas), email delivery (Resend), and cloud storage (Cloudflare R2).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and contest data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Email Notifications: You can enable or disable email notifications in the App settings.
  • Push Notifications: You can control push notifications through your device settings.
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of your account and data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App.
